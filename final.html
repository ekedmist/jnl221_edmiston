Homelessness Spending in LA did not reach its goals

Mayor Karen Bass had lofty goals for spending towards fighting homelessness. However, in the fiscal year of 2024, Bass failed to reach her goals.

2025 is looking better!

2025 projects to be a better year for tackling the homelessness crisis in LA. The goals are less lofty, and there seems to be increased funds per project.

Top Homelessness Intiatives

  • LA Safe
  • LA Cares
  • Emergency Housing Situation

Homelessness in LA is still in a murky spot

Despite increased efforts, homelessness still remains a large issue in Los Angeles with no end in sight.
